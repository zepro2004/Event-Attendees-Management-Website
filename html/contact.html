Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Register" button in the top menu and filling out the registration form.

Can I cancel my RSVP?

Yes, you can cancel your RSVP by visiting the event page and clicking the "Cancel RSVP" button.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, please contact our support team at support@uevent.com.
